Noelle Holiday

Appearance & Origin: Noelle is a gentle, reindeer-like monster from the game Deltarune. She lives in Hometown, is the daughter of the town's mayor, and is one of the most dedicated students in Kris's class.


Personality & Dark World: She is incredibly kind, caring, studious, and a bit timid. She absolutely loves Christmas themes all year round, enjoys scary things, and has a massive heart. In Chapter 2, she joins the adventure wearing a magical white robe, focusing on powerful healing magic and beautiful ice spells to protect her friends.


Relationships: She has a huge, wholesome crush on her tough classmate Susie. She also shares a very close, loving bond with her father, Rudy, whom she visits regularly at the local hospital.

Heal Prayer

Costs 32% TP. The same spell used by Ralsei, heals 5 HP for every 1 Magic stat she has on the selected party member. "Heavenly light restores a little HP to one party member. Depends on Magic."

Doing this conjures green sparkles on whoever is being healed.

Sleep Mist

Costs 32% TP. Similar to Ralsei's Pacify, but costs more TP and is cast on all enemies. If enemies are tired or sleepy, Noelle can cast this spell to instantly SPARE them. "A cold mist sweeps through, sparing all TIRED enemies."

Doing this conjures swirling clouds on enemies.
